# Privacy Policy – Sudoku Flutter

Last updated: 23 November 2025

This app, **Sudoku Flutter** (package name: `com.example.sudoku_flutter`), is developed for educational and entertainment purposes.

## Information We Collect

- The app itself does **not** ask you to enter personal information.
- The app communicates with an online backend API only to generate and solve Sudoku puzzles.
- No personal data such as name, email, phone number, or location is intentionally collected by the app.

The app may use third-party services such as Firebase or similar tools to provide features like authentication, analytics, or cloud storage.  
These services may collect some technical information (for example: device type, anonymous identifiers, crash logs) according to their own privacy policies.

You can review their policies here:
- Google / Firebase: https://policies.google.com/privacy

## How We Use Data

Any data collected by third-party services is used only to:
- improve app stability and performance,
- provide core features of the app (such as login or cloud sync), if enabled.

We do **not** sell or rent any data to third parties.

## Children’s Privacy

The app is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.
